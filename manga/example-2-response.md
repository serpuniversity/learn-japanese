# MANGA TRANSLATION BREAKDOWN

## 1. ORIGINAL
すし屋はあそこだ！
おれが作った最高の店だぞ！

## 2. KANA / FURIGANA / ROMAJI

Kana        | Furigana | Romaji
------------|----------|--------
すし屋      | すしや   | sushi-ya
は          | は       | wa
あそこ      | あそこ   | asoko
だ          | だ       | da
おれ        | おれ     | ore
が          | が       | ga
作った      | つくった | tsukutta
最高        | さいこう | saikō
の          | の       | no
店          | みせ     | mise
だ          | だ       | da
ぞ          | ぞ       | zo

## 3. TRANSLATION

Japanese        | Romaji        | Literal                         | Natural
----------------|---------------|----------------------------------|-------------------------------
すし屋は        | sushi-ya wa   | as for the sushi shop            | the sushi place
あそこだ        | asoko da      | is over there                    | is right there
おれが          | ore ga        | I                                | I
作った          | tsukutta      | made                             | built
最高の          | saikō no      | best                             | ultimate
店              | mise          | shop                             | place
だぞ            | da zo         | (assertive emphasis)             | I’m telling you!

Literal:
"The sushi shop is over there! It’s the best shop that I made!"

Natural:
"That’s the sushi place right there! I built it myself—it’s the best!"

## 4. EDITORIAL

- あそこ = “over there” (visible, some distance away)
- おれ = rough masculine “I”
- 作った = “made/built” (natural for businesses)

Grammar:
- おれが作った最高の店 = “the shop that I made” (relative clause)

Tone:
- だぞ = strong, confident, bragging emphasis

Overall vibe:
Confident, hyped, slightly boastful—he’s proud of what he built.
